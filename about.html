ABOUT GEMH

The Ga East Municipal Hospital was commissioned by His Excellency Nana Addo Dankwah Akuffo Addo on Wednesday 6th November, 2019.
The hospital is a 100-bed facility located in the Ga East Municipality within Kwabenya.

In March 2020, the facility was identified and earmarked as a national treatment center for COVID-19 pandemic. The facility opened up to provide clinical services to the general public in April of 2021.

The hospital is equipped to provide a broad range of services including outpatient, surgery, antenatal and delivery services among others.

Mission
