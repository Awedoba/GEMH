Finance DEPARTMENT

The finance department is responsible for designing and implementing sound financial policies for managing financial activities of the hospital.
The department is headed by the head of finance and currently consists of five(5) functional units namely: Revenue, Expenditure, National Health Insurance Scheme(NHIS), Corporate, Prestige, Financial and Reporting Units.

  • • Head of Finance: The Head of Finance is responsible for the overall management of the department. The head of finance reports directly to the Medical Superintendent and is a member of the hospital managementteam.

    Units

    • Revenue Unit

      The Revenue unit is responsible for billing, receipt and banking of all money or funds accrued to the hospital.

    • National Health Insurance Scheme Unit

      The National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS) Unit is responsible for collating, keying, vetting and timely submission of claims to National Health Insurance Authority(NHIA).

    • Expenditure Unit

      The Expenditure Unit is responsible for processing all payment due suppliers, statutory bodies and the safe keeping of such documents.

    • Financial Monitoring & Reporting Unit

      The Financial Monitoring & Reporting Unit is responsible for assessing and advising on the adequacy and effectiveness of the control system in general and carry out financial data quality assurance.

    • Corporate and Prestige Unit

      The Corporate and Prestige Unit is responsible for processing of private health insurance claim. They responsible for submitting monthly bills to corporate instituions and individuls.
