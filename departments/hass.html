Health Adminstration DEPARTMENT

The Health Administration of GEMH is a division that exists to coordinate and ensure the provision and management of general administrative services in an efficient, effective and fiscally prudent manner to support the day to day operation of the facility. The division can be thought of as the engine that propels the hospital.

Units

  • General Administration

    The General Administration Unit is established to handle the general administrative activities to support the main functions of the hospital. The department plays a central administrative support role to ensure that common administrative services are provided in a uniform manner. It ensures efficient provision and management of administrative services that are capable of contributing to the larger GHS goal of strengthening governance and improving efficiency and effectiveness of the health system.

  • I.T.

    The Information Technology(I.T.) Unit supports the communication and knowledge exchange in the hospital in terms of information communication technology and technologies. The unit supports and manages the software and hardware systems used in the hospital. The unit provides technical support for users and ensures that I.T. resources are used in line with I.C.T. protocols. The unit also trains, maintains, and updates the software and hardware resources used in the hospital.

  • Estate

    This Estate Unit ensures proper integrated planning and budgeting for optimal location, and quality of health facilities. The department provides advice, standard technical norms and management guidelines for contract administration to all levels. In addition, the department ensures acquisition of the right specification and legal ownership of all health estates and maintain database on health estates.

  • Procurement

    Procurement generally refers to the final act of purchasing but it can also include the procurement process overall which can be critically important for the hospital leading up to their final purchasing decision. The hospital can be on both sides of the procurement process as buyers or sellers.

  • Clinical Engineering

    The clinical Engineering department ensures the availability of effective and efficient managerial and technical support for all healthcare equipments at all levels of healthcare delivery in the hospital.

  • Transport

    This unit is designated to handle all emergency and non-emergency transport services. Services may include medical support during journeys, especially for vulnerable clients. Trips can be arranged via phone. Our Drivers are carefully screened to strictly adhere to privacy and confidentiality standards. Once you get on board you are assured of your safety under all conditions.

  • Kitchen/Canteen

    This department mainly deals with the preparation and distribution of meals to clients and staff at a cost.

  • Security

    Security service is responsible for ensuring the safety of hospital properties, personnel, patients and public as well as regulating human and motor traffic within the hospital premises.

  • Social Welfare

    The Social Welfare unit seeks to improve the psychological well-being of patients and other health workers in the hospital setting and to integrate disadvantaged people into the main stream development. They develop strategies to reduce patients length of stay and discharge, advocate medical care for patients to aid in treatment, develop, plan and coordinate patient care and rehabilitation where necessary. This unit also provides assistance to vulnerable persons, persons with diability, homeless people, orphans, etc. to access health care services in the facility without discrimination.

  • Laundry

    The Laundry Unit is responsible for laundering used linens and clothing and redistributes them to the user units on daily bases.

  • Central Supply Sterilization Department

    The Central Supply Sterilization Department (CSSD), also called sterile processing department (SPD), sterile processing, or central supply, is an integrated place in hospitals and other health care facilities that performs sterilization and other actions on medical devices, equipment and consumables; for subsequent use by health workers in the operating theatre of the hospital and also for other aseptic procedures, e.g. catheterization, wound stitching and bandaging in a medical, surgical, maternity or paediatric ward.

  • Mortuary/Pathology Unit

    The mortuary is a place used for the storage of human corpses awaiting identification or removal for autopsy or respectful burial, cremation or other method. In modern times, corpses have customarily been refrigerated to delay decomposition

  • Records/Biostatistics

    This Unit mainly registers patients into the hospital information system. It is the first point of call when a client visits the hospital. This department is responsible for issuing cards and folders to patients, keeping patients' redcords compiling and analysing both clinical and administrative data in the hospital and undertake some research activities.

  • Creche

    This is the nursery where babies and young children are cared for during working days.
