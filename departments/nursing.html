Nursing DEPARTMENT

The nursing department is a specialized department, which is dedicated to patients with specific needs. Each health care provider in the department is well trained with up todate technology to handle all health related situations.

Objectives :

• Training of staff on policies and protocols
• Monitoring and supervision of clinical care
• Promoting quality assurance
• Evaluating clinical audit
• Implementation and evaluation of curative and rehabilitation.

Units

  • OPD

    The Out Patient Department of our hospital is a place where patients are provided quality medical consultations and other allied services. It has a large sitting area where patients can wait before they go int the Consultation chambers where they are provided medical, surgical or allied(physiotherapy, dietetics) consultations and expert opinion. Examination rooms where patients can be examined for any disease condition. Diagnostics which have radiology, pathology, microbiology and other diagnostic services and/or sample collection points. Pharmacy services are also provided to serve patients.

  • ANC/PNC

    One of our priority focus areas is antenatal and postnatal care (ANC and PNC) platforms – both strengthening their foundations in order to meet current and future WHO guidelines, but also innovating both the platforms and the services offered on the platforms to further improve service delivery and quality of care.

  • Wards

    A hundred plus bed facility which houses the following wards:

    • Emergency Ward
    • Male Ward
    • Female Ward
    • Pediatric Ward
    • VIP Ward
  • Eye Clinic

    The Hospital’s Eye Centre provides specialist eye care services and attends to all eye-related diseases it receives in and outside the sub-region.

  • ENT Clinic

    ENT, also known as otolaryngology, specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the ear, nose and throat.

  • ART Clinic

    The Anti-Retroviral therapy (ART) and Counselling unit is in charge of all activities involving HIV/AIDS prevention and management and general counselling for all persons concerning HIV/AIDS and other social issues.

  • Theatres and Recoveries

    Theatres and recovery is the ultimate definition of a multidisciplinary team, working in this challenging and rewarding unit.It requires skilled and knowledgeable individuals to come together and work in sophisticated and innovative ways to ensure patient safety, patient experience and excellent patient outcomes.

  • Family Planning

    Our family planning unit aims to provide all primary prevention programs, fertility awareness creation and sensitization, guidanc/counseling support to couples and individuals who seek to create a well planned family lifestyle.

  • Public Health

    Public health aims to improve the quality of life through prevention and treatment of disease, including mental health. This is done through the surveillance of cases and health indicators, and through the promotion of healthy behaviors. Common public health initiatives include promotion of hand-washing and breastfeeding, delivery of vaccinations, suicide prevention, smoking cessation, obesity education, increasing healthcare accessibility and distribution of condoms to control the spread of sexually transmitted diseases.
