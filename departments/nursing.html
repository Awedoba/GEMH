Nursing DEPARTMENT

A nursing department is a special department, typically in a hospital setting, dedicated to patients with specific needs. Each health care worker in the department is trained to care for specific situations or types of patients.

Objectives of the Nursing Department includes:

• Training of staff on policies and protocols
• Monitoring and supervision of clinical care
• Promoting quality assurance
• Evaluating clinical audit
• Implementation and evaluation of curative and rehabilitation.

Units

  • OPD

    The Out Patient Department of our hospital. It is the section of the hospital where patients are provided medical consultations and other allied services. It has following parts and services Consultation chambers where patients are provided medical, surgical or allied(physiotherapy, dietetics) consultation and expert opinion. Examination rooms where patients can be examined for any disease condition. Diagnostics which have radiology, pathology, microbiology and other diagnostic services and/or sample collection points Pharmacy which provides medications to the patients.

  • ANC/PNC

    One of our priority focus areas is antenatal and postnatal care (ANC and PNC) platforms – both strengthening their foundations in order to meet current and future WHO guidelines, but also innovating both the platforms and the services offered on the platforms to further improve service delivery and quality of care.

  • Wards

    These are the wards that are available in Our hospital totaling into a total of 100 + beds

    • Emergency Ward
    • Male Ward
    • Female Ward
    • Pediatric Ward
    • VIP Ward
  • Eye Clinic

    The Hospital’s Eye Centre provides specialist eye care services and attends to all eye-related diseases it receives in and outside the sub-region.

  • ENT Clinic

    ENT, also known as otolaryngology, specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the ear, nose and throat.

  • ART Clinic

    The Anti-Retroviral therapy (ART) and Counselling unit is in charge of all activities involving HIV/AIDS prevention and management and general counselling for all persons concerning diseases and certain social issues.

  • Theatres and Recoveries

    Theatres and recovery is the ultimate definition of a multidisciplinary team, working in this challenging and rewarding unit.It requires skilled and knowledgeable individuals to come together and work in sophisticated and innovative ways to ensure patient safety, patient experience and excellent patient outcomes.

  • Family Planning

    Family planning, natural: Also known as fertility awareness, periodic abstinence and the rhythm method, this approach entails not having sexual intercourse on the days of a woman's menstrual cycle when she is most likely to become pregnant or using a barrier method (such as a condom, the diaphragm or a cervical cap) for birth control on those days.

  • Public Health

    The division responds to the public health aspects of major incidents or disasters, monitors health, identifies trends and evaluates the impact of health services. The division is responsible for improving health through measures that prevent disease and injury.
